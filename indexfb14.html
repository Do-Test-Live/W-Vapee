INVITATION -- Shenzhen Ecig Expo 2019

Shenzhen eCig Expo (IECIE) 2019 will be the 5th edition of the show based in Shenzhen, the headquarter of e-cig industry. The first IECIE was held in 2015 and the show has expanded to 40,000 ㎡ within just five years. IECIE 2019 is expected to attract 50,000 professional visitors and 1,500 brands from 45 countries. IECIE covers the scales of e-cig finished products, liquids, atomizer, hardware, packaging, accessories, solutions and components, aiming to be the world class influential international e-cig exhibition.
